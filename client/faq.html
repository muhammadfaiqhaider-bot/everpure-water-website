Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver across Rawalpindi and Islamabad, covering 15+ localities. Check our Service Areas page to see if we cover your neighborhood yet.

How do I place an order?

Simply visit our Order page, choose your bottle size and quantity, and submit your details. Our team will confirm your delivery slot shortly after.

Do you provide dispensers?

Free water dispenser for offices, call centers, and customers with a daily consumption of 10 or more bottles.

Can I order for school function?

Absolutely. We supply bulk orders for offices, schools, and events with flexible delivery schedules tailored to your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery as well as major mobile wallet transfers. Bank transfer is also available for bulk/office orders.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help — reach out anytime.
